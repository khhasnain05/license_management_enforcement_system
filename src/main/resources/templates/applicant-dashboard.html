🪪 Apply for Learner Permit

ℹ What is a Learner Permit?
A Learner Permit is a temporary permit that allows you to practice driving under supervision. It is valid for 6 months and is the first step toward a driving license.
After applying you will need to:
📎 Upload CNIC copy, passport photo, and medical certificate
📝 Pass the computer-based theory test (70% minimum)
🚗 Pass the practical driving test
💳 Pay the license issuance fee

🔄 Apply for License Renewal

ℹ Renewal Requirements
You will skip the Learner and Theory Test phases. Please ensure you have your old license number and a fresh medical certificate ready to upload.
